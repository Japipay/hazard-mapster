About the Website

This website provides a comprehensive Hazard Map of Zamboanga City, encompassing all 98 barangays within the city. It systematically presents information on prevalent natural hazards, including flooding, landslides, earthquakes, and coastal-related risks, allowing users to identify potential threats specific to each barangay. The platform is designed with a clear and structured layout to ensure accessibility, accuracy, and ease of understanding for all users.

In addition, the website includes barangay-specific emergency hotline numbers to support timely communication and response during disaster situations. Its primary purpose is to enhance public awareness, disaster preparedness, and risk reduction by centralizing critical hazard and emergency information in a single, reliable source. Through this platform, residents, stakeholders, and visitors are better equipped to make informed decisions and respond effectively to natural hazards in Zamboanga City.

About This Project

We are students of the Ateneo de Zamboanga Senior High School that aspires to centralize the information for emergency risks and emergency hotlines for Zamboanga City and most of its barangays. We embody that goal through this website that provides local hazard risks such as flooding or landslide risks per barangay as well as emergency hotlines for that barangay.

Hazard Mapster was developed to provide residents and officials of Zamboanga City with accessible, comprehensive hazard information for all barangays across District 1 and District 2.

The platform aims to:

  • Increase public awareness of local hazards
  • Support emergency preparedness planning
  • Enable informed decision-making for residents and businesses
  • Assist local government units in disaster management
  • Promote community resilience and safety
